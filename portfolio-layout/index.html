What is Performance design

Its a very specialised job. Performance design is a process of creating a character out of plain words.

As we know that in films and tv serials, if the characters are unique and innovative, they immediately connect with the audience and create history. A story revolves around characters, characters are not just written words, its a living being and if we fail to translate the written character into that living being, our efforts go wasted. Decoding authors vision is our main motive. We design subconscious of a character, their drive, their history, their inspiration, aspirations, motivations.
